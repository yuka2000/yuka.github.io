HOST FAMILIES WANTED!!

Help shape the future of Japanese exchange students and create memorable experiences.

Improvement in Language Skills

Talking daily with the host family improves language skills, especially in English. Real-life use helps students express themselves accurately and naturally.

Understanding of Culture

Living with a host family teaches students about local culture beyond textbooks. Practical experiences enrich their learning.

Local Support

Host families, as locals, support students adjusting to a new environment. They provide advice and help when needed.

Cultural Exchange

Interaction with host families facilitates cultural exchange. Sharing home country customs and learning local lifestyles creates meaningful experiences.

Emotional Support

Host families act as a supportive "family," easing feelings of isolation. A comfortable environment is vital for student confidence and growth.

Cross-Cultural Understanding

Through the host family, students develop skills in understanding and accepting different backgrounds and values. This fosters cross-cultural understanding, contributing to a broader international perspective.
